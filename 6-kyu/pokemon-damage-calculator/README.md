<h2><a href=https://www.codewars.com/kata/536e9a7973130a06eb000e9f/train/javascript target="_blank">Pokemon Damage Calculator</a></h2><h3>6 kyu</h3><p>It's a Pokemon battle! Your task is to calculate the damage that a particular move would do using the following formula (not the actual one from the game):</p><pre><code class="language-javascript"><span class="cm-variable">damage</span> <span class="cm-operator">=</span> <span class="cm-number">50</span> <span class="cm-operator">*</span> (<span class="cm-variable">attack</span> <span class="cm-operator">/</span> <span class="cm-variable">defense</span>) <span class="cm-operator">*</span> <span class="cm-variable">effectiveness</span></code></pre><p>Where:</p><ul><li>attack = your attack power</li><li>defense = the opponent's defense</li><li>effectiveness = the effectiveness of the attack based on the matchup (see explanation below)</li></ul><p>Effectiveness:</p><p>Attacks can be super effective, neutral, or not very effective depending on the matchup. For example, water would be super effective against fire, but not very effective against grass.</p><ul><li>Super effective: 2x damage</li><li>Neutral: 1x damage</li><li>Not very effective: 0.5x damage</li></ul><p>To prevent this kata from being tedious, you'll only be dealing with four types: <code>fire</code>, <code>water</code>, <code>grass</code>, and <code>electric</code>.  Here is the effectiveness of each matchup:</p><ul><li><p><code>fire &gt; grass</code></p></li><li><p><code>fire &lt; water</code></p></li><li><p><code>fire = electric</code></p></li><li><p><code>water &lt; grass</code></p></li><li><p><code>water &lt; electric</code></p></li><li><p><code>grass = electric</code></p></li></ul><p>For this kata, any type against itself is not very effective. Also, assume that the relationships between different types are symmetric (if <code>A</code> is super effective against <code>B</code>, then <code>B</code> is not very effective against <code>A</code>).</p><p>The function you must implement takes in:</p><ol><li>your type</li><li>the opponent's type</li><li>your attack power</li><li>the opponent's defense</li></ol>
